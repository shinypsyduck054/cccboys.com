How the math works

The calculator does two separate calculations: wage (guaranteed by the corps general fund, paid weekly) and surplus (paid quarterly out of what's left from your products' accounts after the waterfall).

wage = hours_per_week × tier_rate × 52 weeks

Surplus is approximate. The model treats each app's revenue (Supporter unlocks plus its share of Patron dues) as flowing through the waterfall: 20% operating, then 15% corps cross-subsidy on what remains. The remaining cash supports wages plus surplus. We estimate the wage burn on those apps at 40% of revenue (a draft assumption) and the rest becomes the surplus pool. Your share is proportional to your contribution.

per_app_revenue = (supporters × $1) + (patrons × $12 / catalog_size)
total_revenue = per_app_revenue × apps_you_built
surplus_pool = total_revenue × 0.68 − (total_revenue × 0.40)
your_surplus = surplus_pool × (contribution_share / 100)

The wage burn assumption (40%) and the operating + subsidy percentages (20% + 15%) are draft. The waterfall order is the load-bearing part, not the exact percentages. See the neobank page for the full waterfall.
