    How the model works
    Deterministic, not stochastic

    Each strategy choice tunes a set of rates: apps shipped per month, probability of breakout per app, probability of press hit per month, probability of grant landing per quarter. The model walks month by month, accumulating expected revenue and headcount under those rates.

    Catalysts are placed at deterministic months based on expected wait time (1 / probability). When a catalyst lands, it adds revenue, shifts the trajectory, and shows up in the event log. Members get hired as cash position supports them.

    Survival check: green if corps reached cash-positive within horizon, yellow if borderline (within 30% of break-even), red if losing money at horizon end.
